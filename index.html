What is Lost Plot?

About Lost Plot

Lost Plot is a vintage fashion project that curates unique second-hand and vintage pieces, blending sustainable style with personal expression. Born as an online outlet on Depop run by a small team and created by Kathleen Eng, it’s grown into a community-driven space featuring styled outfit reels, pop-up events, and storytelling through fashion. With a focus on individuality, timeless aesthetics, and thoughtful curation, Lost Plot invites you to explore style that’s expressive, conscious, and ever-evolving.
